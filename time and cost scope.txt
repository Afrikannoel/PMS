- **Duration:** 2-3 weeks

  - Implement user forms for drug management, sales, and reports.

- **Duration:** 3-4 weeks
- **Key Tasks:**

  - Implement notifications for low stock and expiring drugs.

### **Phase 4: Testing & Integration**
- **Duration:** 2-3 weeks
- **Key Tasks:**

  - Debug and ensure smooth functionality across different user roles.

- **Time Investment:** ~40-60 hours.

### **Phase 5: Deployment & Documentation**
- **Duration:** 1-2 weeks
